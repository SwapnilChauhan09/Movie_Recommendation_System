# Movie_Recommendation_System


Have you ever, 

- Quickly found the exact product that you were looking up on Amazon.
- Everybody at the high school is talking about this new cool Netflix series "squid game" and when you open Netflix, the first thing
that you find on the home screen is the same "squid game".
- You just finished that amazing funny sitcom "Friends" and you got a suggestion to watch another similar sitcom "HIMYM"
- Got a recommendation for the second part of harry potter after completing the first one?

Well, these are just some very basic examples of recommendation systems in action!

As we are addressing the rapid growth in data collection in recent years, there are countless ways where these amazing data could be utilized.
Let's explore one of its applications which makes our life easier by showing the exact things that we search for or recommending the things 
that we might like. 

The recommendation systems, are the type of information filtering systems as they improve the search results and provide the items that are more relevant to us. They are now part of our day-to-day life from Youtube and Netflix to Amazon.

The recommendation system plays are very important role, as the success of the product depends on the effectiveness of its recommendation system, E.g. Spotify, Youtube, Amazon..  etc

More relevant content results in better user engagement and leads to growth for the product.

Now let's explore the tip of the iceberg by following the three methods for a recommendation system.

1. Demographic Filtering
2. Content Based Filtering
3. Collaborative Filtering
